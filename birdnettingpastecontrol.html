Long-Lasting, Unobstructed Bird Control Networks

Pigeons and birds nesting on window panes or balconies create significant health risks from dropping accumulations, alongside causing persistent structural odor problems. Traditional bulky containment methods compromise architectural looks, making light-weight, heavy-strength nylon networks a premium modern priority.

Our high-density copolymer wire integrations are anchored cleanly with stainless steel robust hooks, ensuring no loose ends or hanging segments develop under extreme tropical weather or severe monsoons.

Product Features

  • 100% humane, non-toxic barrier system
  • Highly resistant to rust, rot, and severe heat
  • No view blockage or ventilation issues
  • Heavy-grade stainless steel framing setups

Our Anchoring and Installation Process

01
Shaft Mapping

Our engineers trace window profiles, structural pipes, and perimeter lengths carefully.

02
Frame Anchoring

Heavy stainless steel structural anchors are drilled systematically along outer masonry lines.

03
Tension Tuning

Nets are stretched smoothly over anchors to block bird paths permanently without sagging.
